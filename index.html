About

Imagine looking into a mirror and having your reflection speak to you in your voice. That pretty much describes what you’ll experience on the Goodangel platform.

Added as goodies to make your Digital twin more meaningful is a messaging system that allows users to record voice messages that are then sent by their digital twins.

Also a folder management system that is hosted by a user’s digital twin allows for the creation, organization and sharing of video, image and article playlists, that are generated as a result of user answers to their Digital twin’s prompts.

Best of all, Goodangel’s account creation process includes users providing government identification to be crosschecked using facial and voice recognition. As a security protocol, this guarantees a user’s authenticity, providing the most optimum solution for securely harnessing this wonderful technology.

AI create your Digital Twin with just a selfie and voice sample.

Messaging system

Chatbot

Digital Twin managed folder management system

Through prompts delivered by a user’s digital twin, users are able to have video, image and article playlists created and added to folders users will have access to, and will also be able to share with other users.
